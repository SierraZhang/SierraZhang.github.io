Creative side

As the end of last semester approached, I did not expect that the final project of the art course would be to create our own music video in groups of two. This was challenging because we found that we knew nothing about music making, and the only photography tool we owned was a phone with terrible picture quality. It was hard for me to imagine how we could complete the project.

Nevertheless, there is always a way forward. The music making part went quite smoothly. However, when it came to the video making, everything became much tougher. Creating an original story was itself challenging, not to mention the difficulty of making it into a video with limited technical skills and equipment. Besides that, the time available for us was quite short. I also found that the higher the quality I demanded of the video, the heavier the burden on me became. When inspiration was gone, there was nothing I could do about it. I could not sleep well during those nights because I could not stop thinking about the content of the script. I watched many music videos that had similar concepts to the one I was working on. Finally, on one morning, my imagination and inspiration came together and I finished the whole script: two girls lost in their spiritual world, how they tried to find each other through empathy, etc. I was shocked by the many novel concepts I came up with. Next, we decided to film the video in a park which had the conditions that we needed for the story. We prepared a case of props and costumes from things that we found in our home and we used them to dress ourselves. We made full use of the scenery in the park. Later, I learned editing techniques and added special effects to make the scenes better fit the concepts I had in mind.

This experience reminds me how marvellous my creativity can be. I realised that creativity comes from a mixture of aptitude and effort. It can help me to fully utilise the things I have available. Limited resources canâ€™t stop me from successfully completing a challenge like this.
