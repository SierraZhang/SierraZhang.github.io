Creative side

As the end of last semester approached, I did not expect that the final project of the art course would be to create our own music video in groups of two. This was challenging because we found that we knew nothing about music making, and the only photography tool we owned was a phone with terrible picture quality. It was hard for me to imagine how we could complete the project.

Nevertheless, there is always a way forward. The music making part went quite smoothly. However, when it came to the video making, everything became much tougher. Creating an original story was itself challenging, not to mention the difficulty of making it into a video with limited technical skills and equipment. Besides that, the time available for us was quite short. I also found that the higher the quality I demanded of the video, the heavier the burden on me became. When inspiration was gone, there was nothing I could do about it. I could not sleep well during those nights because I could not stop thinking about the content of the script. I watched many music videos that had similar concepts to the one I was working on. Finally, on one morning, my imagination and inspiration came together and I finished the whole script: two girls lost in their spiritual world, how they tried to find each other through empathy, etc. I was shocked by the many novel concepts I came up with. Next, we decided to film the video in a park which had the conditions that we needed for the story. We prepared a case of props and costumes from things that we found in our home and we used them to dress ourselves. We made full use of the scenery in the park. Later, I learned editing techniques and added special effects to make the scenes better fit the concepts I had in mind.

This experience reminds me how marvellous my creativity can be. I realised that creativity comes from a mixture of aptitude and effort. It can help me to fully utilise the things I have available. Limited resources can’t stop me from successfully completing a challenge like this.

可以使用下面的公式求一元二次方程 ax2+bx+c=0ax2+bx+c=0 的两个根：

r1=−b+b2−4ac⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯√2a,r2=−b−b2−4ac⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯√2ar1=−b+b2−4ac2a,r2=−b−b2−4ac2a

其中 b2−4acb2−4ac 被称作一元二次方程的判别式（可记作 ΔΔ），如果 Δ>0Δ>0，那么一元二次方程有两个不同的实数根，如果 Δ=0Δ=0，则方程有两个相等的实数根，如果 Δ<0Δ<0，则方程没有实数根。

请编写程序，让用户输入 a,b,ca,b,c 的值，然后在有实数根的情况下解这个方程并打印结果，如果没有实数根，请打印该方程没有实数根的提示。

对任何一个正整数 n，如果它是偶数，那么把它砍掉一半；如果它是奇数，那么把 (3n+1) 砍掉一半。这样一直反复砍下去，最后一定在某一步得到 n=1。卡拉兹在 1950 年的世界数学家大会上公布了这个猜想，传说当时耶鲁大学师生齐动员，拼命想证明这个貌似很傻很天真的命题，结果闹得学生们无心学业，一心只证 (3n+1)，以至于有人说这是一个阴谋，卡拉兹是在蓄意延缓美国数学界教学与科研的进展……

我们今天的题目不是证明卡拉兹猜想，而是对输入的任一不超过 1000 的正整数 n，简单地数一下，需要多少步（砍几下）才能得到 n=1？

有一份 Iris Data Set 数据集，是数据分析领域一项经典的分类数据集，它包含了若干朵鸢尾花 (Iris) 的花瓣长度、花瓣宽度、花萼长度、花萼宽度的数据，以及这些鸢尾花各自所属的种类 (Setosa、Versicolor、Virginica 三种中的一种)。

当我们遇到一朵新的鸢尾花时，可以基于这些数据来猜测新的鸢尾花所属的种类。

能实现这种任务的算法有很多，我们来尝试自己实现 kNN (K-NearestNeighbor) 算法来完成本项任务。kNN 算法是一种简单的分类算法，它的步骤是：

  1. 根据特征数据，计算新输入的样本与数据集中所有样本的距离
  2. 根据距离排序，筛选出距离最近的 kk 个样本
  3. 从中取出现次数最高的类别作为对新输入的样本预测的分类

我们简化一下问题，假设只通过花瓣的长度和宽度来给鸢尾花进行分类，举例展示一下计算步骤：

When it came to parts that none of us could work out, we looked for video resources and made notes from the videos. I still found the naming principles for organic substances tricky, so I turned to my school teacher for help.

As a result of these new efforts, I found that my knowledge of chemistry significantly improved and I started to pay more attention to details which were skipped by me before as I did not have enough awareness through independent study. I am not saying that self-study is not an effective method. In my opinion, the most efficient way to learn is to ask for an appropriate amount of help based on your personal thinking and understanding of the study topics. Other people may have more experience than you and may be able to offer better suggestions to assist you in your study.

Drama Club

Audition

Preparation

• I have always had a dream about acting since a very young age. At the beginning of this term. We got a chance to choose the club we liked. And for Drama Club , everyone is required to pass the audition for being a club member. Before I had never done the acting in front of many people’s staring eyes. But I did have the strong longing to act. So I decided to give a shot .

• 1 week before the audition, the original club leader sent us the scripts as the audition’s content .

• I decided to chose the male role as I wanted to try to transpose. Later ,when I was doing the preparation，I found the content and the spirit of it was quite difficult to understand.I even watched the video of professional actors and actress acting the play. Then I practiced again and again till I think there’s no improvement I could make .However due to the limited time, I didn’t really remember all the words from the scripts when I was about to join the audition.

Audition

• When I entered the audition room, the club leader and a few original club members had already sat there in a big circle waiting for us. I chose a seat to sit down with nervous in my heart. The audition began, the two guys unexpectedly did the brilliant job. They acted without looking at the scripts and acted like the professional actresses who had been in their career for several years. I could sense the emotions and feelings from their acting. Finally they all got very high praise from the club leader. Then it was my turn.All of the sudden, I really felt my heart was about to jump out of my mouth. But I forced myself to calm down and never lose heart. I had to took my scripts with my shaking hand and walked slowly to the middle of the circle. I first bowed and started my acting. It was not that bad as I thought. In the first moment I started to act the first scene，I suddenly calmed down and got into the atmosphere. I acted as I was the man using all his feelings and words to show his love to the girl. The struggled feeling bumped in the air around me. When it came to the second scene, I acted as Romeo in the famous Shakespeare play. It was the self-chosen script and I had to act in English. I was not as good as the two girls before but at least I did my best and didn’t shrink back. The club leader then made her commentary. She said in the first scene, I tried to make the eye contact with the audience and that was a good thing. However in the second scene my English pronunciation was a little bit strange but that was fine because I could take the vocalization exercise in the future. Also, The fact that I wasn’t off scripts affected my performances in someways. I took her suggestions by heart and was grateful for this amazing chance this audition offered.
